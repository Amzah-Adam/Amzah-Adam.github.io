Synopsis


Dr James P. Orwell, a brilliant scientist, is a survivor of the destruction of recent man-made climate change. After leaving the safety of his bunker in search of equipment to help repair it, he encounters a terrible, unforgiving post apocalyptic world. He must fight the elements, among other things, in this harsh landscape- sculpted by pollution and climate change.

 

This film is proudly presented by climate community. We have strived to produce a scientifically accurate film, showing people the dangers of what might be to come.
